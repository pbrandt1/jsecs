The evil MR RATFELLER has unleashed a horde of robot mice in San Francisco to steal from the working class.

Hardware hackers and makers from across the city have banded together to create a rival fleet of robot cats to catch and destroy the robot mice.

Unfortunately, no software develoeprs were available to write the A.I. for the feline fleet, as every last software dev was attending a popular conference in the area. The rag tag team of hackers instead created a simple algorthm based on genetics. The cats behavior is entirely governed by a gene sequence, which controls which way the cat moves. When a cat runs into a robot mouse, it eats the mouse and absorbs its battery level. When a cat runs into another cat, they combine their genes and load those new genes into a new cat, and split their battery levels. WHen a cat runs out of battery, it has to wait for another cat to run into it.
