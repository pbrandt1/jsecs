The evil MR RATFELLER has unleashed a horde of robot mice in San Francisco to steal from the working class.

Engineers from across the city have banded together to create a rival fleet of robot cats to catch and destroy the robot mice.

Unfortunately, no software develoeprs were available to write the A.I. for the feline fleet, as every last software dev was attending a popular conference in the area. The hardware engineers instead created a simple algorthm based on genetics. The cats behavior is entire governed by a gene sequence, which controls which way the cat moves. When a cat runs into a robot mouse, it eats the mouse and absorbs its battery level. When a cat runs into another cat, they combine their genes and load those new genes into the cat with the lower battery level. WHen a cat runs out of battery, it has to wait for another cat to run into it.
